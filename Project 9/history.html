Cat History:


In Egypt

Egypt is said to be the site of domestication for cats, between 4000 and 2000 BC.

Egyptians worshipped cats, as they were said to be a symbol of grace and poise.

Midieval Times

Cats still held an important role for humans - mostly for their mice catching abilities.

There was also a large population of people in different cultures who believed cats were a sign of the devil.

Supersitions

Many cultures have supersitions about cats. There is a belief that cats have several lives (number of lives depends on the country.)

Depending on the culture, the superstitions are possitive or negative.

Black cats are considered good luck by The Scots, Britain, Japan, and the creator of this website.

Most of Europe considers the black cat a symbol of bad luck.

Black Cat Appreciation Day is August 17th. (Not a superstition.)
