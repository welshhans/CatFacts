Reasons to Adopt

  • Approximately 2.6 million cats (and dogs) are euthanized in U.S. shelters each year.
  • Cats (and dogs) are still being purposely bred in factory-style facilities. They are raised from birth in horrible conditions such as improper medical care; The mothers are kept in cages without human contact for several years, and more. If you buy an animal from a pet store, you are most likely getting it from a breeding facility.
  • You'll save thousands of dollars. The fees for adopting an animal are significantly lower (and sometimes free) versus buying a special breed from a pet store or breeder.
  • Many cats you find at shelters are already housetrained.
